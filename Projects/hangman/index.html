Instructions

  1. This is a two player game
  2. Before starting take a look at the game set-up and if you forget the instructions
    you can click on the instructions icon in the middle to view them again
  3. Once both players are ready player one will click the start button to begin the game
  4. Once the game has began player one's timer will begin and player one has to guess a letter
    as quickly as possible
  5. Once player one guesses a letter and if that letter is in the word it will appear on the screen
    if not then one of the body parts for the hangman will be drawn

    6. *Once player one has clicked a letter player one's timer will stop and player two's timer will begin

  6. Each player will have a turn to guess a letter until all the hangman body parts are drawn
  7. At any time in the game any player can guess the word
  8. If a player guesses incorrectly he/she will no longer be able to play and the other player will play
    until the hangman is drawn
  9. If neither player is able to guess the word then it is a draw
  10. If a player guesses the word correctly the game ends and that player is the winner. Their name and time
    will be recorded on the leaderboard.

Have fun playing :)
